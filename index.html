About Us

Mining Infrastructure Specialists

Mortom Projects & Construction is a 100% black youth-owned specialist in mining infrastructure. We deliver engineering, construction, logistics, and supply services across Africa's mining sector.

Our culture of ingenuity and structured methodology ensure safe, on-time delivery under even the most challenging conditions.

We specialize in a wide range of services, from mining civil works to electrical installations, building construction, and logistics support.

Our Services

Our Services

Mining Civil Works

Comprehensive civil engineering solutions for mining operations.

  • Tailings dams, slurry pipelines
  • Mineral-processing foundations
  • Bulk earthworks, cut-and-fill, access roads
  • Ore-handling systems

Civil Works & Infrastructure

Building the foundational infrastructure for communities and industries.

  • Roads, bridges, interchanges
  • Drainage systems
  • Dams, canals, reservoirs
  • Water-treatment structures

Electrical & Instrumentation

Comprehensive electrical solutions for mining and industrial applications.

  • High & low-voltage reticulation
  • Switchgear & substations
  • SCADA, PLC integration
  • Site camps electrical systems
  • Shutdown commissioning

Building & Modular Accommodation

Flexible and durable building solutions for remote locations.

  • Processing plants, workshops, offices
  • Control rooms
  • Modular housing, ablution blocks
  • Site camps

Logistics & Plant Hire

Comprehensive logistics and equipment solutions.

  • Heavy-lift cranes & mobile workshops
  • Oversized cargo transport & fleet management
  • Warehousing & shutdown support
  • Mobile plant washing services

General & Mining Supplies

Reliable supply chain for mining and construction needs.

  • Bulk cement, aggregates, piping, valves
  • Fasteners
  • PPE, safety barriers, signage
  • Fencing

Waterproofing & Coatings

Comprehensive waterproofing solutions for various applications.

  • Residential waterproofing
  • Commercial waterproofing
  • Dam wall construction
  • Protective coatings & linings
